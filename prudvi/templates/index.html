How BloodFlow Works

🧑‍⚕️

Donor

Register, complete medical eligibility check, and schedule your donation. Your blood group is matched to urgent requests.

↓
🏦

Blood Bank

Central hub: collects, stores, tracks every blood unit with expiry dates and chain of custody. Updates inventory in real-time.

↓
🚗

Volunteer

Accepts transport assignments, picks up blood units from the blood bank, and delivers to hospitals. GPS tracked throughout.

↓
🏥

Hospital

Submits priority blood requests, tracks real-time volunteer location, confirms receipt, and provides feedback.

👨‍💼 Admin supervises the entire system — approves requests, manages inventory, assigns volunteers, and generates analytics.
